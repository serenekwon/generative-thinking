# Generative Thinking

Generative Thinking helps you maintain cognitive sovereignty in the age of AI by using AI to build, use, and maintain your own thinking framework.

This repository is both the guide and the working environment for Generative Thinking.

Serene Human Framework is the first reference example with Generative Thinking.

---

## What You Can Do

- Start designing your own thinking framework with AI.
- Find a central model from recurring questions and experiences.
- Organize and maintain your thinking like a document repository.
- Use, adapt, or revise Serene Human Framework as a reference example.

## Where to Start

This repository has one central purpose and two possible paths.

The purpose is to maintain your cognitive sovereignty in the age of AI by building your own thinking framework, starting from self-understanding.

Path 1 is to start from your own questions and experiences.

Path 2 is to read, apply, or revise Serene Human Framework as a reference example.

Generative Thinking can be used in two stages.

```text
Questioning / discussion stage
- Human: methods/how-to-question.md

Documentation / editing stage
- Human: methods/how-to-edit.md
- AI reference principles: AGENTS.md
```

There is no separate AI conversation instruction for the questioning stage.

AI becomes useful through conversation as it gradually learns the user's taste, perspective, thinking style, habits, and recurring patterns.
