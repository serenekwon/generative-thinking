# Manifesto

AI amplifies human thought.

That means the central question of the AI era is not only technology, but humanity.

I choose not to fear AI.

Instead, I choose to better understand people.

What makes people move?

Why do some people come alive while others burn out?

Why do we search for meaning, misunderstand one another, and see the same world in different ways?

In pursuit of these questions, I built a thinking framework.

And if AI is a tool that expands human thought, then the process of building that framework may also help someone else.

That is why I decided to make this repository public.

I am not trying to publish only a few finished essays.

I want to preserve how those essays began from specific questions and experiences, how they became connected into a thinking framework, and how AI helped implement that framework as writing and documents.

I call this repository the Generative Thinking Repository.

Generative Thinking helps you maintain cognitive sovereignty in the age of AI by using AI to build, use, and maintain your own thinking framework.

Self-understanding is one of the most important starting points for maintaining that sovereignty.

You can start from your own questions and build your own thinking framework.

This repository is both the guide and the working environment for that system, and Serene Human Framework is its first example.
